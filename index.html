LIVING ON THE LAND

Western Cape's Outdoor Experience

WHY LIVE ON THE LAND?

When caos of the city life gets too much to handle and you need a break from dad to day traffic and nothing but the sound of technology all around you, escape into the wild and live alongside Western Capes beautiful nature and stay the night or the weekend.

Become one with nature and give yourself that break you need with many outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, mountain climbing, canoeing, camping and hunting. So what are you waiting for. Your gateway to freedom awaits.
