LIVING ON THE LAND

Western Cape's Outdoor Experience

WHY LIVE ON THE LAND?

When caos of the city life gets too much to handle and you need a break from day to day traffic and nothing but the cbsound of technology all around you, escape into the wild and live alongside Western Capes beautiful nature and stay the night or the weekend.

Become one with nature and give yourself that break you need with many outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, mountain climbing, canoeing, camping and hunting. So what are you waiting for. Your gateway to freedom awaits.

ACTIVITIES

FISHING

The Western Cape area has much to offer the keen angler. There are many spots, rock and surf, close to each other, so a fishing getaway will have many opportunities, both for a change of scene, and “luck”.
The most common fish found in this area are Steenbras (throughout the year) and kabeljou (in the summer,) with galjoen, elf and snoek in season. A highlight for anglers is the crayfish/rock lobster season in summer (usually November to January) when these delicacies are caught during the limited and strictly-monitored period.

CANOEING

Kayaking and canoeing can be either relaxing or exhilarating, depending on the area you choose. It’s a great way to explore the Cape, allowing you to drift along the water, take in the scenery and keep a sharp eye out for wildlife and birds.
Kayaking in Cape Town offers an amazing opportunity to explore the lovely creatures that inhabit the deep blue waters including penguins, whales, dolphins, and dozens types of fish species from a kayak.
